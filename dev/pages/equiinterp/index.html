AutoBZ.EquiBaryInterp.LocalEquiBaryInterp — Type
LocalEquiBaryInterp(x::AbstractVector, y::AbstractVector, [degree=8])
LocalEquiBaryInterp(x::Vector, y::Vector, w::Vector, h)

Construct a local barycentric Lagrange interpolant that forms a degree degree local polynomial approximation of the data y on the equispace grid x, which must be identical to a range with step size h. w are the equispace interpolation weights.
